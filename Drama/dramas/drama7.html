Money Heist

Summary

Money Heist (La Casa de Papel) is a Spanish heist crime drama series created by Álex Pina. It follows the story of a group of robbers led by "The Professor" who plan and execute heists on the Royal Mint of Spain and the Bank of Spain.

Cast

  • Álvaro Morte as The Professor / Sergio Marquina
  • Úrsula Corberó as Tokyo / Silene Oliveira
  • Itziar Ituño as Raquel Murillo / Lisbon
  • Pedro Alonso as Berlin / Andrés de Fonollosa
  • Alba Flores as Nairobi / Ágata Jiménez

Storyline

The series explores themes of resistance, love, and betrayal as the robbers face off against the authorities and deal with personal conflicts within their group.
